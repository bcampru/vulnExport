Version 8847

NOTICES 

Importance When What Detail
High May 21, 2024 We intend to introduce TSIDs for new App-IDs that we will use to identify traffic related to creating, deleting, and sharing files in Microsoft OneDrive Business: ms-onedrive-business-create, ms-onedrive-business-delete, and ms-onedrive-business-share. These TSIDs will help you prepare for these new App-IDs before we activate them on June 18, 2024.
NOTE: SSL decryption is required to identify this traffic. 		Link
High May 21, 2024 We intend to introduce TSIDs for a variety of Google applications (Google AppScript, Google Chat functional apps, Google Docs functional apps, Google Drive functional apps, Google Forms, Google Meet functional apps, and Google Sheets). These TSIDs will help you prepare for these new App-IDs before we activate them on June 18, 2024.
NOTE: SSL decryption is required to identify this traffic. 		Link
Medium  May 21, 2024 We intend to expand coverage for the github-base App-ID to identify traffic associated with github.io (currently identified generally as ssl and web-browsing).
NOTE: SSL decryption is not required to identify this traffic. 		Link
Medium  May 21, 2024 We intend to expand coverage for the sway App-ID to identify traffic associated with Sway (currently identified generally as ssl and web-browsing).
NOTE: SSL decryption is required to identify this traffic. 		Link
Medium  May 21, 2024 We intend to expand coverage for the dropbox-editing App-ID to identify traffic associated with editing the name or content of files in Dropbox using the MS Word embedded solution (currently identified generally as sharepoint-online-uploading and web-browsing).
NOTE: SSL decryption is required to identify this traffic. 		Link
Medium  July 16, 2024 We intend to activate the Marijuana category that we added to the Advanced URL Filtering/PAN-DB category list in content version 8838.
NOTE: This new category is visible on all PAN-OS releases but we support it only on PAN-OS 9.1 and later versions. 

[ACTION REQUIRED] If you currently alert on or block marijuana websites using a combination of the Health and Medicine and Abused Drugs categories, you need to set the action for this new Marijuana category to “Alert” or “Block” as appropriate before we activate this category. After we activate this new Marijuana category, you will no longer be able to use the category combination of Health and Medicine with Abused Drugs to identify and act on marijuana-related websites. 		Link 
Informational  May 21, 2024 We intend to release new App-IDs. As always, customers are encouraged to leverage an Application Filter to adopt new App-IDs. Link

 		May 21, 2024 We intend to release new ICS, IoT, and OT placeholder App-IDs (amx-icsp, simatic-logon-encrypted-login, and simatic-logon-unencrypted-login). We then intend to activate (add signatures for) these new App-IDs in the new App-IDs Applications and Threats content update scheduled for June 18, 2024. Link
Informational  May 21, 2024 We intend to activate (add signatures for) the following ICS, IoT, and OT App-IDs: mitsubishi-hmi, simatic-logon, and the honeywell-softmaster functional App-ID (all introduced as placeholder App-IDs in content version 8837). Link
